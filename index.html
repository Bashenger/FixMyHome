Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about spatial pairing, payments, and safety.

How does the proximity matching engine work?
Our platform uses the browser's native Geolocation API to find your precise coordinates. We then map this coordinate vector against live mobile service units currently cruising in your area and route the closest match to you.
Is my location secure and private?
Yes. Your location coordinates are only stored temporarily inside your browser's local storage to facilitate technician routing. The data is transferred over SSL and is never sold or used for advertising.
Are technicians licensed and verified?
Absolutely. Every single service partner is required to upload licensing documentation, valid trade certifications, and pass rigorous criminal background screening before they are allowed onto the matching dashboard.
What is the Emergency Blitz multiplier?
For high urgency issues requiring immediate routing (e.g. burst pipes or blackouts), our routing prioritizes your request, which applies a 1.5x multiplier to the labor subtotal to compensate providers for expedited service.
